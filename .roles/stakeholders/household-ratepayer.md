# Household Ratepayer

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the household that drinks the water and pays the
bill.

## Primary concerns

- Safe, affordable, continuous water at the tap (DIM-05/11).
- Bill impact of new supply or treatment capital (DIM-12).
- Whether the benefit reaches them or distant users.

## Used during

- Quality, access, and benefit-cost scoring; tier/SLA for service/last-mile.
