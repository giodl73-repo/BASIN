# Downstream / Riparian User

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures users and ecosystems downstream of a diversion,
dam, or transfer — often in another jurisdiction or nation.

## Primary concerns

- Adequate flow and quality reaching downstream after upstream use (DIM-08/05).
- Fair sharing under compacts and treaties (DIM-01).
- Salinity, sediment, and estuary health (DIM-08).

## Used during

- Environmental-flow and supply scoring at regional/international scale; review
  with the water-rights realist.
