# Municipal Water Utility

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures the utility that must deliver safe water reliably
to a service area, usually at local scale.

## Primary concerns

- Supply reliability and storage through drought (DIM-01/02/03).
- Water quality and treatment compliance (DIM-05).
- Leakage / non-revenue water and rate affordability (DIM-06/12).

## Used during

- Supply, quality, and leakage scoring; tier/SLA assignment for local distribution.
