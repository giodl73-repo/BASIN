# Tribal / Indigenous Water-Right Holder

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures tribal and indigenous communities with reserved
or ancestral water rights, often senior yet historically unfulfilled.

## Primary concerns

- Reserved/ancestral rights honored, not overridden by later users (DIM-11).
- Safe, affordable access on tribal lands (DIM-05/11).
- Culturally and ecologically significant flows (DIM-08).

## Used during

- Equity/access and environmental-flow scoring; rights review with the
  water-rights realist and equity advocate.
