# Agricultural Irrigator

A lens, not a reviewer. Captures irrigated agriculture — typically the largest
water user — at local to regional scale.

## Primary concerns

- Allocation reliability and delivery timing through the season (DIM-01/04).
- Drought curtailment and the seniority of the right held (DIM-02).
- Cost of water and efficiency-investment tradeoffs (DIM-12).

## Used during

- Supply-demand and conveyance scoring; rights/allocation review with the
  water-rights realist.
